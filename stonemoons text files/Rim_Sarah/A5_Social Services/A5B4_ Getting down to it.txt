To:   Sarah MurphyFrom:   SarahSubject:   Psych filesDateLine:   Fall 1993The funny thing was when this all started, I thought I could count on people. Obviously, I reasoned, the Social Services, those boogy monsters of my childhood, were really reasonable (although slightly overworked) human beings. They could direct me to places to get training, help for Laurel. After all, dear, they are professionals, some nurse or other told me.But they didn't know. When I called upon the power of social services, the hydra came alive—each head spitting a different venom:  *   Abandonment*   Censure*   Helplessness*  Freudian Blame##higher##lower___________________Sarah/ Moon/ Laurel___________________
